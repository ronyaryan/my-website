What are the Subjects used to study Astrononmy

Hi everyone! My name is Rony Aryan and Today I am going to tell you what are the subjects used to study astrononmy and what are the uses of the subjects.

Subjects used to study astronomy :

1.Physics
2.Mathematics
3.Chemistry
4.Computer Science
5.Astrophysics
6.Cosmology
7. Planetary Science

Uses

Physics : This is the backbone of astronomy. You will learn about motion, gravity, light, and energy everything needed to understand how planets, stars, and galaxies behave.
Mathematics : Astronomy uses a lot of math (especially algebra, calculus, and statistics) to calculate orbits, distances, and physical properties.
Chemistry : Helps explain what stars and planets are made of and how elements form in space.
Computer Science : Used for simulations, data analysis, and handling large datasets from telescopes.
Astrophysics : Focuses on the physical processes of celestial objects like black holes, stars, galaxies.
Cosmology : Looks at the origin, evolution, and fate of the universe.
Planetary Science : Covers planets, moons, and solar systems.
